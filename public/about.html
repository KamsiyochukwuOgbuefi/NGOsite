Our Identity

We are a humanitarian organization dedicated to supporting survivors of violent attacks in vulnerable Christian communities across Nigeria. Our mission is to restore hope, dignity, and stability to individuals and families affected by terrorism by providing emergency relief, trauma care, community rebuilding, and long-term recovery support. Grounded in compassion, accountability, and respect for human life, we work to ensure that every survivor—regardless of background—has access to safety, healing, and opportunity. We partner with local leaders, faith communities, and international supporters to protect at-risk populations, empower displaced families, and help rebuild communities impacted by violence. Above all, we stand with survivors, advocating for their rights and helping them reclaim their future with strength and resilience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide compassionate, life-restoring support to Christian survivors of terrorist attacks in Nigeria. We work to deliver emergency relief, trauma healing, shelter, education assistance, and long-term community recovery. Through advocacy, protection, and empowerment, we strive to help survivors rebuild their lives, restore dignity, and create safer, stronger communities where hope can flourish again.
